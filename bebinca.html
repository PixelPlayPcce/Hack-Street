Bebinca

Bebinca is a traditional Goan dessert, often referred to as the "Queen of Goan desserts." It is a rich, layered cake made primarily from ingredients like coconut milk, sugar, ghee (clarified butter), egg yolks, and flour. The dessert is known for its unique texture, which is soft, slightly chewy, and fragrant, with alternating layers of caramelized goodness.
