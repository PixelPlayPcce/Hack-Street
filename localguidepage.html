Fish Curry Rice

A staple in Goan cuisine, this aromatic curry is made with coconut, spices, and fresh fish, served with steamed rice.

Vindaloo

A spicy and tangy curry originally from Portuguese influence, typically made with pork or chicken.

Xacuti

A complex curry made with roasted spices and coconut, usually prepared with chicken or lamb.

Bebinca

A traditional Goan dessert made with layers of coconut milk, eggs, and flour, baked to perfection.

Humann (हुमण)

Fish curry, also known as Kadī (कडी) or Āmbat (आंबट), served with steamed rice (शीत/भात). A classic Goan dish.

Fried Fish (तळील्लें नूस्तें)

Crispy and flavorful fried fish, a popular side dish in Goan cuisine.

Fish Suke (सुकें नूस्तें) or Dhabdhabit

A dry spicy preparation of fish, served as a side dish. Known for its intense flavors.

Fish Udid Methi (उद्दमेथी)

A type of curry consisting of fenugreek and mackerel. A vegetarian version is also prepared using hog plums or raw mango.
